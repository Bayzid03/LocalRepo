Instructions to Purchase Tickets

(1) Tickets can be bought online ten days in advance.

(2) You can pay for the tickets using mobile financial services: Bkash, Nagad, Rocket

(3) In case of payment or transaction failure, the deducted amount would be refunded by your bank or MFS provider within 8 business days.

(4) If you have not received your ticket copy in email, kindly check your Spam / Junk folder.

Please help Bangladesh railways and government of Bangladesh in moving towards a digitized and cashless economy. Eradicate black money.
